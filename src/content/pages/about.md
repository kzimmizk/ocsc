## Our Mission

Ouray County Soccer Club (OCSC) is dedicated to developing confident and
competitive athletes with an emphasis on respect and personal growth. We teach
and promote the game through quality coaching and abundant playing
opportunities. We are committed to teaching beautiful, tactical soccer while
making sportsmanship and respect for teammates, opposing players, and referees a
primary focus.

## Who We Are

OCSC is a volunteer-run non-profit based in Ridgway, Colorado, supporting youth
across Ouray County. The program is run by volunteers at both the board and
coaching levels. We offer two seasons each year — **Fall** and **Spring** — both
providing outdoor playing opportunities.

As a sanctioned club affiliated with the Colorado Soccer Association, OCSC is
automatically affiliated with US Youth Soccer, US Soccer, and FIFA, with access
to the resources of CSA and the Colorado Soccer Referee Program.

## Where Registration Fees Go

All registration proceeds go toward the growth of the club, including training
and background checks for our volunteer coaching staff, league and referee fees,
field maintenance, player insurance and CSA fees, and equipment.
